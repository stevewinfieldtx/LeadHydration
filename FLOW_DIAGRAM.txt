                              KEY PRINCIPLE

   Industry Detection MUST happen before Pain Point Mapping because:

   ❌ WRONG: "Your solution addresses manual processes"
      → But manual WHAT? Scheduling? Order entry? Time tracking?
      → Depends entirely on the industry!

   ✅ RIGHT: "Machinery companies struggle with manual production scheduling"
      → Industry-specific pain point
      → Leads to industry-specific questions
      → Matches industry-specific references
